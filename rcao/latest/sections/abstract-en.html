Abstract

The Resilient Collective Awareness Ontology (RCAO) provides a formal model for representing collective awareness in the automated driving domain. It extends established ontologies like PROV-O and the Automotive Global Ontology (AGO) to describe the state of awareness of different traffic participants (vehicles, drivers, infrastructure), its provenance, and associated quality attributes such as trust and resilience. RCAO defines different levels of awareness (perceptual, comprehension, projection) and the cognitive processes that generate them, enabling a detailed, machine-readable representation of dynamic traffic scenarios. **The latest OWL encoding of the RCA Ontology can be found [here](http://example.org/pplan.owl)**
